Web Development

Web development involves creating websites and web applications. It encompasses front-end development (user interface), back-end development (server-side scripting and databases), and full-stack development (both front-end and back-end). Developers use languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Django. They ensure responsive design for various devices, follow best practices for performance and security, and adapt to emerging technologies for better user experiences. Web development is vital in shaping the online world, making content and services accessible to people worldwide.
